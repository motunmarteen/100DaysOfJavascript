Create a program that checks if a year is within the 21st century.

The 21st century refers to the current era we are living in, spanning from January 1, 2001, to December 31, 2100. It's the period of time following the 20th century, marked by significant advancements in technology, communication, globalization, and various societal changes.

This century has witnessed rapid technological innovations, such as the widespread adoption of the internet, the proliferation of smartphones, advancements in artificial intelligence, breakthroughs in medicine, renewable energy developments, and much more.

Culturally, the 21st century has seen shifts in values, entertainment, and societal norms, with increased awareness and activism on social issues, a surge in online connectivity and social media usage, and a focus on sustainability and environmental concerns.

Each century often carries its own defining characteristics, and the 21st century is expected to shape the future in significant ways across various aspects of human life.
